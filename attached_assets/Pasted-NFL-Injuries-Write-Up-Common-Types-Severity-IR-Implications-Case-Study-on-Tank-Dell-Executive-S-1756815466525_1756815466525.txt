Severity Levels and Recovery Timelines
Severity is graded (mild/Grade 1: minor swelling; moderate/Grade 2: partial tears; severe/Grade 3: full ruptures), influencing return-to-play. 3 7 11 14 General vibes for Tiber:

Injured Reserve (IR) Placements
IR is heartbreaking—players must miss at least four games, and season-long designations (e.g., for ACL tears) crush depth. 5 In 2024, ~500 players hit IR, with teams like the Eagles and Chiefs heavily affected. 0 9 2025 preseason saw early IR hits (e.g., Panthers WR Jalen Coker, legendary OL Will Clapp out for season). 24 Return from IR: Up to eight designations per team, but severe cases (e.g., multi-ligament knees) often mean full-year absence. 5 Fantasy vibe: IR stashes can pay off, but severe injuries tank value.
